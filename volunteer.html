TAMAN JURONG MAKEFEST 2019

My team and I were tasked to teach the residents in Taman Jurong about the functionalities of the Google Home Mini.

We had a list of basic functions and added in our own few like:

  • "Ok Google, set all lights to 50%."
  • "Ok Google, play my rock and roll playlist on Spotify."

MERDEKA GENERATION ROADSHOW

We get to teach seniors on some of the apps done by the Government like myCPF, HealthHub and Singpass Mobile. They also get to ask us regarding their phones like "how to connect to the public WiFi".

For example, we taught the seniors that the Singpass Mobile app helps to access any Singpass related sites easier. They can do that just by scanning the QR code instead of typing in the username and password with the OTP.

Fei Yue Senja House Painting & Spring Cleaning

Over 3 months at ITEHQ, I managed to complete 6 projects as part of their digitalisation move to improve and streamline the processes.

We were given the chance to clean and paint the homes of the seniors living in Senja. My team and I were tasked to spring clean a senior's house. We were given mops, sponges, detergent and etc, and managed to clean the whole house in about 3 hours.
